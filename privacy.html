Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Welcome to ToneFixer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at tonefixer.online.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Text you submit: When you use our tone rewriting tool, we process the text you enter. This text is sent to our AI service to generate results.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous data such as pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type through Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience and serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI tone rewriting service
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

3. Text Processing & AI

The text you submit to ToneFixer is processed by Claude AI (by Anthropic) to generate rewritten content. We do not permanently store the text you submit. Text is only used in real-time to generate your results and is not used for training AI models.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI text processing
  • Google Analytics — for website analytics
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Vercel — for website hosting

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We do not store your submitted text on our servers after processing is complete.

7. Children's Privacy

ToneFixer is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: tonefixer.online/contact
