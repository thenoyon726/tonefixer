Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ToneFixer

Is ToneFixer free to use? +
Yes! ToneFixer is completely free to use with no signup required. You can rewrite texts instantly without creating an account. For unlimited rewrites and all 6 tone styles, upgrade to Pro for just $5/month.
What languages does ToneFixer support? +
ToneFixer works with 50+ languages including English, Bengali, Spanish, French, Arabic, Hindi, Portuguese, German, Japanese, and many more. Simply paste your text in any language and the AI will rewrite it in the same language with the chosen tone.
Is my text kept private and secure? +
Absolutely. Your text is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We do not save, share, or use your content for any purpose other than generating the rewritten result. Your privacy is our top priority.
What are the 6 tone styles? +
ToneFixer offers 6 professional tone styles: Professional (polished and confident, perfect for business emails), Friendly (warm and approachable for colleagues), Formal (serious and structured for official documents), Casual (relaxed and conversational for chats), Persuasive (compelling for sales and pitches), and Empathetic (caring and understanding for sensitive messages).
How does ToneFixer work? +
ToneFixer uses Claude AI — one of the most advanced language models in the world — to understand the meaning and context of your text, then rewrites it in the tone you select. The AI preserves your original meaning while transforming the style, vocabulary, and structure to match the chosen tone perfectly.
What types of text can I rewrite? +
You can rewrite virtually any type of written communication — emails, messages, cover letters, proposals, apologies, social media posts, product descriptions, reports, and more. ToneFixer works for both personal and professional communication in any language.
What is the difference between Free and Pro? +
The Free plan gives you access to the core ToneFixer tool with no signup required. The Pro plan ($5/month) unlocks unlimited rewrites, all 6 tone styles, support for 50+ languages, an ad-free experience, and priority support. Pro is ideal for professionals who communicate frequently and need perfect tone every time.
Can I use ToneFixer for commercial purposes? +
Yes! ToneFixer can be used for professional and commercial communication including business emails, client proposals, marketing copy, and customer service messages. Many of our users are freelancers, entrepreneurs, and business professionals who use ToneFixer daily for their work communication.
