Frequently Asked Questions

Is ToneFixer free to use?
Yes! ToneFixer offers free rewrites every day. For unlimited access, check out our Pro plan at just $5/month.
Does ToneFixer store my text?
No. Your text is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.
What languages does ToneFixer support?
ToneFixer works with 50+ languages including English, Bengali, Spanish, French, Arabic, Hindi and many more.
How does the AI work?
ToneFixer uses Claude AI by Anthropic — one of the most advanced language models available — to understand and rewrite your text in the chosen tone.
Can I use ToneFixer for commercial purposes?
Absolutely! Many of our users use ToneFixer for business emails, marketing copy, and professional communication.
