Our Story

ToneFixer started with a simple observation: brilliant people were losing opportunities because their written communication didn't reflect their true capabilities. A great idea poorly worded in an email, a heartfelt apology that came across as cold, a confident person whose messages sounded uncertain.

We built ToneFixer to be the communication companion everyone deserves — smart, fast, and always available. No sign-up required, no complicated settings. Just paste your text, pick a tone, and let AI do the magic.

Today, ToneFixer helps thousands of people across 120+ countries communicate more effectively every day — from students writing college applications to professionals closing business deals.
